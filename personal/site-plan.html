Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to show and document my graphic design portfolio and freelance rates. The goal is to demonstrate and generate business either for job applications or freelance opportunities.

Audience

My audience is B2B business strategies at the small business and local events for freelancing work while also catering to anyone looking to hire for fulltime work. Although my fulltime audience is rather wide, my primary focus is in the freelance side of my work.

Dynamic elements

The dynamic elements will primarily consist of pop-up ads for my side projects on Youtube and Twitch alongside my Zazzle shop for additional internal advertising. This will ensure all aspects of my buisness is being displayed even if not all parts of my side hussles are relevant for the project.
